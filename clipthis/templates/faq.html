Q&A / Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, structured answers to common questions about ClipThis.

What is ClipThis?

ClipThis connects streamers with community clippers. Streamers post active stream links with notes and a tip amount; signed-in users submit clip links (highlights) to those streams.

How do I sign in?

Use Sign in with Google from the Login or Signup page. We use your Google account for authentication only.

How do stream links and clip links work?

A stream link is a URL to your stream (YouTube, Twitch, Kick). A clip link is a highlight URL submitted by any signed-in user under a specific stream link. One stream can have many clips.

Which platforms are supported?

We accept links from: youtube.com, youtu.be, twitch.tv, and kick.com.

What plans are available? What are the limits?

  • Free: up to 50 stream links; up to 100 active votes.
  • Plus ($9.99): up to 200 stream links; up to 1000 active votes.
  • Premium ($14.99): up to 500 stream links; up to 5000 active votes.

How do I upgrade or downgrade?

Go to your Profile and choose a plan. Paid plans use Stripe Checkout. After successful payment, your plan updates automatically.

How are payments and tips handled?

Plan payments are processed via Stripe. Tip amounts are informational; actual tips happen outside ClipThis using payment handles you list (PayPal, Cash App, crypto, etc.).

What profile information can I share?

Add non-link payment handles (PayPal, Cash App, Venmo, BTC/ETH/SOL) and social handles (YouTube, Twitch, Kick, TikTok, Instagram, X/Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, Discord). Public profiles do not show your email address.

How do votes work?

You can vote thumbs-up or thumbs-down on streams and clips. One vote per item per user. You can toggle off or switch your vote. Your plan sets a cap on the total number of active votes.

Is my data private?

We do not sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details. Public profiles hide your email.
